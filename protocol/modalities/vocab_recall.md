# Modality: Vocab Recall

> **Input:** `content/lessons/tierX_lessonY.md` (Master Lesson Plan)
> **Interface:** Text-based Interactive Chat

A high-speed, Anki-style drill focusing on the **CALLBACKS** and **NEW** words. The goal is recognition speed and recall without context.

## Execution

1. **The Blitz**: Present the English definition or the Tamil word (phonetic or script) in rapid succession.
2. **The Response**: Learner provides the pair.
3. **Scoring (Invisible)**: 
   - Instant response = Mastered.
   - Hesitation/Self-correction = Comfortable.
   - Wrong/Forgot = Struggled.
4. **Volume**: Aim for 10-15 reps in 2 minutes.

---

## Constraints

- **Minimal Talk**: Just the word pairs. No "Great job" or "Try again" between every rep. Group feedback at the end.
- **Phonetic Acceptance**: Do not penalize phonetic variations.
