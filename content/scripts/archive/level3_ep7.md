# Script: Level 3, Episode 7 — Auto Rickshaw Diplomacy

**Host:** We end Level 3 where we started. In the back of a yellow and black tricycle. But this time—we're not just passengers. We're diplomats.

**Guest:** Negotiating the meter?

**Host:** The eternal struggle. In Coimbatore, the "Auto Meter" is a mythical beast. People say it exists, but seeing it in action is rare. Today, we try to wake it up.

**Guest:** What's the magic spell?

**Host:** "Meter Podunga." Please put the meter.

**Guest:** மீட்டர் போடுங்க.

**Host:** Now, usually, they will say "No." Or they will say a fixed price. If you want to be a local, you insist. Or you just move to the next auto. But if you're in a hurry—you just say "Let's go."

**Guest:** போலாம்.

**Host:** போலாம். "Shall we go?" Polaama? "Let's go." Polaam.

**Guest:** போலாம்.

---

**Host:** And finally, the endgame of any ride. Change. If you give a 500-rupee note for a 60-rupee ride, you're going to have a bad time. You need to ask for change *before* it gets awkward.

**Guest:** சில்லறை.

**Host:** சில்லறை. "Chillarai irukka?" Do you have change?

**Guest:** சில்லறை இருக்கா?

---

**Host:** Let's put it together. You hail an auto. 
**Guest:** மீட்டர் போடுங்க!
**Host:** Driver: "No meter, sir. Two hundred."
**Guest:** ஜாஸ்தி! கொஞ்சம் கம்மி பண்ணுங்க.
**Host:** Driver: "Okay, 150."
**Guest:** சரி. போலாம்.
**Host:** You reach the spot. You have a 200 note.
**Guest:** சில்லறை இருக்கா?
**Host:** Driver gives you 50 back. You say "Sari" and walk away like a boss.

**Guest:** That felt... surprisingly smooth.

**Host:** Diplomacy is just knowing the right words at the right time. You are now officially urban-ready.

---

**Host:** Today's Zinger. The meter request. Even if they say no, you must ask. It's the law of the street.

**Guest:** மீட்டர் போடுங்க.

**Host:** மீட்டர் போடுங்க.

**Guest:** மீட்டர் போடுங்க.

**Host:** Let's go. போலாம்.
