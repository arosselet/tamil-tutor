# Tier 2, Mission 29 — The Audit Under the Table

**Andrew (M):** Okay, Thambi. We need to find it. The box. Where is it?

**Thambi (M):** அண்ணா, I don't know! It's gone!

**Andrew (M):** I know you're scared, Thambi. பயம் வேண்டாம். Don't be scared. We'll find the பெட்டி. 

**Thambi (M):** சரி... Let's search.

**Andrew (M):** Okay, first, the Table. Is the box here? பெட்டி இங்க இருக்கா?

**Thambi (M):** இல்ல அண்ணா. இங்க இல்ல. Look under the Table. டேபிள் கீழ பாருங்க.

**Andrew (M):** Under? Okay... [Sound of chair moving] ... கீழ பாக்குறேன். No, nothing here. Just dust.

**Thambi (M):** Maybe it's up there? Fridge மேல பாருங்க! 

**Andrew (M):** The Fridge? Up there? Okay, let me check... [Sound of climbing a stool] ... Fridge மேல? No. இங்கயும் இல்ல. Nothing up here but an old newspaper.

**Thambi (M):** அய்யோ! எங்க போச்சு? Where did it go?

**Andrew (M):** Wait, Thambi. You said 'First look there, then look here.' முதல்ல அங்க, அப்புறம் இங்க. You're directing me.

**Thambi (M):** No, அண்ணா! I'm helping! முதல்ல table கீழ, அப்புறம் fridge மேல... that's a good plan, right?

**Andrew (M):** Something is wrong. ஏதோ தப்பு இருக்கு. You're too nervous.

**Thambi (M):** No தப்பு! Everything is fine. I'm சந்தோஷம்! I'm happy we are searching!

**Andrew (M):** You're happy? சந்தோஷம்? You just said you were scared. பயம். Now you're happy? 

**Thambi (M):** I... I... okay, maybe it's under the sofa? சோபா கீழ பாருங்க!

**Andrew (M):** No, Thambi. I can't do this anymore. என்னால முடியாது. I can't find it if you keep lying.

**Thambi (M):** அண்ணா, தப்பு! I'm not lying! 

**Andrew (M):** Then why are you looking at the curtain? 

**Thambi (M):** [Quietly] No... don't look there...

**Andrew (M):** Wait. There's something near the curtain. A small piece of plastic. It's a clue. ஏதோ இருக்கு...

**Thambi (M):** அண்ணா, please! வேண்டாமே! 

**Andrew (M):** First the table, then the fridge... you were trying to keep me away from the corner. முதல்ல table, அப்புறம் fridge. But the பெட்டி is close. I can feel it.

**Thambi (M):** [Whimpers] பயமா இருக்கு...

[Pause: 3 sec]

**Analyst Maya (F):** Did you hear that tension? Andrew and Thambi are literally tearing that dining room apart. 

**Analyst Raj (M):** Yeah, and Thambi is playing a very dangerous game with directions. He’s trying to steer Andrew everywhere *except* where that box is.

**Analyst Maya (F):** Exactly. Before we dive into the mystery, Raj, did you catch the word for 'under'?

**Analyst Raj (M):** I did. It showed up right at the start of the search. 
**Thambi (M):** "டேபிள் கீழ பாருங்க."
**Analyst Raj (M):** "டேபிள் கீழ பாருங்க." Look under the table. That word is `கீழ`. 

**Analyst Maya (F):** `கீழ`. It’s such a fundamental word. You’ll hear it in every house in Coimbatore. "Keep the bag `கீழ`." "It’s `கீழ` the chair." 

**Analyst Raj (M):** And then, to throw Andrew off, Thambi sends him to the exact opposite place. 
**Thambi (M):** "பிரிட்ஜ் மேல பாருங்க!"
**Analyst Raj (M):** "பிரிட்ஜ் மேல பாருங்க!" Look above the fridge! Or on top of the fridge. That’s `மேல`.

**Analyst Maya (F):** `மேல`. So we have `கீழ` for down or under, and `மேல` for up or on top. 

**Analyst Raj (M):** `கீழ` and `மேல`. Andrew even says it back to confirm. 
**Andrew (M):** "கீழ பாக்குறேன்."
**Analyst Raj (M):** "I'm looking under." And later, "Fridge `மேல`?" Up on the fridge?

**Analyst Maya (F):** But the star of the show today is the object they are looking for. The `பெட்டி`.

**Analyst Raj (M):** The `பெட்டி`. It just means 'box.' But in this story, it’s not just any box. 
**Andrew (M):** "We'll find the பெட்டி."
**Analyst Maya (F):** Right. `பெட்டி`. You can have a 'tool `பெட்டி`' or a 'jewelry `பெட்டி`'. It’s the container for all the secrets right now.

**Analyst Raj (M):** Now, Thambi's search strategy was very specific. He used two words to sequence the search. 
**Thambi (M):** "முதல்ல அங்க, அப்புறம் இங்க."
**Analyst Raj (M):** "First there, then here." 

**Analyst Maya (F):** `முதல்ல`. That means 'First.' You've heard this before, but today it’s the key to the timeline. `முதல்ல`. First.

**Analyst Raj (M):** And then `அப்புறம்`. 
**Analyst Maya (F):** `அப்புறம்`. 'Then' or 'Afterwards.' `முதல்ல table கீழ, அப்புறம் fridge மேல.` First under the table, then on top of the fridge.

**Analyst Raj (M):** It’s a classic distraction technique. Andrew starts to catch on, though. He says, "Something is wrong." 
**Andrew (M):** "ஏதோ தப்பு இருக்கு."
**Analyst Raj (M):** `தப்பு`. That means 'wrong' or 'mistake.' In this context, it’s like "something is fishy." `தப்பு`. 

**Analyst Maya (F):** And Thambi tries to cover it up with a big lie. He says he's happy!
**Thambi (M):** "I'm சந்தோஷம்!"
**Analyst Maya (F):** He uses that word we learned in the Boss Fight. `சந்தோஷம்`. Happy. But Andrew isn't buying it.

**Analyst Raj (M):** Because Thambi is clearly still acting out of `பயம்`. Fear. He even admits it at the very end. 
**Thambi (M):** "பயமா இருக்கு..."
**Analyst Raj (M):** "It is fearful" or "I am scared." 

**Analyst Maya (F):** Poor Thambi. But Andrew is losing patience. He uses a heavy negative word. 
**Andrew (M):** "என்னால முடியாது."
**Analyst Maya (F):** "I cannot." Or "It is not possible for me." `முடியாது`. 

**Analyst Raj (M):** `முடியாது`. This is the ultimate "No" in Tamil. It’s not just "no," it’s "I can't do it." "It’s impossible." 

**Analyst Maya (F):** So, to recap our search directions: `கீழ` for under, `மேல` for up. 

**Analyst Raj (M):** `முதல்ல` for first, `அப்புறம்` for then. 

**Analyst Maya (F):** And we are looking for the `பெட்டி`. The box.

**Analyst Raj (M):** But Andrew found something. Near the curtain. `ஏதோ இருக்கு...` "Something is there."

**Analyst Maya (F):** I have a feeling this search is about to get very real. Andrew is getting closer to the corner Thambi was trying to hide.

**Analyst Raj (M):** `முதல்ல` the table, `அப்புறம்` the fridge... but now, the corner. Let's see what Andrew finds in the next mission.
