# Tier 2 Mission 20: The Arrival Dispute (Breakdown)

**Analyst Maya:** (Teaser) Listen for the word for 'angry'. Someone in this room is definitely losing their cool. 

[Intercept Plays]

**Analyst Raj:** (Replay Hook) Let's go back... "அது என் தப்பு இல்ல!" Did you catch that? He's defending himself. "தப்பு" — mistake. 

**Analyst Maya:** Wow, talk about a homecoming. One minute you're arriving at the door, the next you're in the middle of a full-blown family query. 

**Analyst Raj:** Welcome to the breakdown. This scenario is all about friction. We saw some high-stakes words today that really drive a point home. 

**Analyst Maya:** The first one is a big one: **கோவம்** (angry/anger). Akka says "எனக்கு ரொம்ப கோவம் வருது" — I am getting very angry. 

**Analyst Raj:** And Mama's defense? **தப்பு** (mistake/wrong). "அது என் தப்பு இல்ல" — That is not my mistake. 

**Analyst Maya:** Notice how they're using these words to establish blame. **கோவம்** and **தப்பு**. 

**Analyst Raj:** And when Akka doesn't believe them, she asks: **நிஜமா?** (Really? Is it true?). It's a great "fact-check" word for any conversation. **நிஜமா?**

**Analyst Maya:** We also saw two structural words that our learner has been wrestling with: **சொல்றேன்** and **கேக்குறேன்**. 

**Analyst Raj:** "நான் சொல்றேன்" — I am telling/saying. Thambi is repeating this because nobody's listening to him! 

**Analyst Maya:** And when Mama says "தம்பி கேக்குறான்ல," he's using the "asking/questioning" frame. **கேக்குறான்**. 

**Analyst Raj:** These aren't just verbs; they're the connectors of a dispute. I say, you ask, it's a mistake, I'm angry. 

**Analyst Maya:** Let's look at the "Living Tamil" here too. Did you notice how Akka said **வர்றீங்க**? In the textbook, it's "வருகிறீர்கள்." But in the living room, it's just **வர்றீங்க**. Collapsed and fast. 

**Analyst Raj:** And **வந்தாச்சுல்ல** — It's done, right? "வந்தாச்சு" for "arrived/finished" plus that "ல்ல" tag for "right?". 

**Analyst Maya:** Let's do our drill. Raj? 

**Analyst Raj:** Okay. How do you say... "I am angry"? 

[Pause]

**Analyst Maya:** **எனக்கு கோவம்**. எனக்கு கோவம். 

**Analyst Raj:** How about... "Is it really true?" 

[Pause]

**Analyst Maya:** **நிஜமா?**. நிஜமா? 

**Analyst Raj:** And... "My mistake." 

[Pause]

**Analyst Maya:** **என் தப்பு**. என் தப்பு. 

**Analyst Raj:** Perfect. You've navigated the living room minefield. Now, go eat something before they start another argument. 

**Analyst Maya:** See you in the kitchen!
