# Tier 2 Mission 13: The Stream (Say & Ask Remix)

## Word Status Sheet

- **TEACH:** சொல்றேன் (I'm saying), சொல்லுவேன் (I will say), கேட்டேன் (I asked), கேக்குறேன் (I'm asking), கேட்பேன் (I will ask)
- **USE:** சொன்னேன் (I said), அண்ணா (elder brother), அக்கா (elder sister), அவங்க (they/he/she respectful), ஆமா (yes), இல்ல (no), சரி (okay), வணக்கம் (hello)
- **CALLBACK:** இன்னைக்கு (Today), முதல்ல (First), முன்னாடி (Before)

---

## 1. The Wind Down

**Host:** வணக்கம், Agent. 
**Host:** Tonight is a low energy mission. The Stream. 
**Host:** If you are on your evening walk, just keep a steady pace. If you are sitting on the couch, sink into it. Let your shoulders drop. 
**Host:** There are no tests tonight. No high pressure scenarios. Just let the sound of the language wash over you. We are going to build the engine slowly. 
**Host:** You told me earlier today that one word felt comfortable. Solid. "I said". 
**Guest:** சொன்னேன்.
**Host:** Exactly. 
**Guest:** சொன்னேன்.
**Host:** That "-en" ending. It is your anchor. It means "I". The past is fixed. It is done. 
**Host:** You have the past. Now we build the rest of the timeline around it. 

[Pause: 4 seconds]

## 2. The Slow Flow (Saying)

**Host:** Bring your mind to the present. The breath is moving. The words are leaving your mouth right now. 
**Host:** I am saying. 
**Guest:** சொல்றேன்.
**Host:** The sound shifts from 'nen' to 'reen'. It vibrates in the present. 
**Guest:** சொல்றேன்.
**Host:** Try saying it to Akka. Right now. I am telling Akka. 
**Guest:** அக்கா கிட்ட சொல்றேன்.
**Host:** See how it flows. No effort. 
**Guest:** அக்கா கிட்ட சொல்றேன்.
**Host:** Today, I am saying. Use your callback for 'today'.
**Guest:** இன்னைக்கு சொல்றேன்.
**Host:** The past: சொன்னேன். 
**Host:** The present: சொல்றேன். 
**Host:** Let it echo. 
**Guest:** சொன்னேன். சொல்றேன்.

[Pause: 4 seconds]
