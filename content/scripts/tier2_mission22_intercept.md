# Intercept: Tier 2, Mission 22 — The Preference Protocol

**SCENE START**

**[SFX: Clinking of stainless steel plates, the sound of a ceiling fan whirring, someone serving rice.]**

**Amma:** Andrew, உட்காருங்க (sit down). Today special lunch.

**Andrew:** சரி, Amma. Thanks.

**Deepa:** Amma, wait. Don’t serve too much rice.

**Amma:** இல்ல இல்ல, Andrew eats well. இதோ, Pavakkai (Bitter Gourd) roast. It’s very healthy.

**Deepa:** Amma, wait. I don’t think Andrew... Andrew, உனக்கு Pavakkai பிடிக்குமா? (Do you like Bitter Gourd?)

**Andrew:** [Hesitates] Uh... Deepa, எனக்கு Pavakkai... பிடிக்காது. (To me, Bitter Gourd... I don't like.)

**Amma:** என்ன? (What?) Why? Pavakkai is good for the blood.

**Deepa:** It’s okay Amma. உனக்கு வேற என்ன பிடிக்கும்? (What else do you like?)

**Andrew:** எனக்கு Chicken Curry... ரொம்ப பிடிக்கும்! (I really like Chicken Curry!)

**Amma:** [Slightly mollified] சரி, சரி. Chicken Curry நிறைய (a lot) சாப்பிடுங்க.

**[SFX: TV in the background playing a loud movie trailer with heavy percussion. "RA-JI-NI!" chant.]**

**Amma:** அண்ணா! (Brother! - referring to Rajini). TV-ல பாருங்க. New படம் trailer.

**Deepa:** Amma is a huge Rajini fan, Andrew.

**Amma:** Andrew, உனக்கு Rajini படம் பிடிக்குமா? (Do you like Rajini movies?)

**Andrew:** ஆமா Amma. எனக்கு Rajini ரொம்ப பிடிக்கும். He is the Superstar!

**Amma:** [Happy] அப்படீங்களா? நிஜமா? (Is that so? Really?) Very good. உனக்கு வேற எந்த actors பிடிக்கும்? (Which other actors do you like?)

**Andrew:** Uh... I don't know many names. ஆனா Rajini style எனக்கு ரொம்ப பிடிக்கும்.

**Deepa:** [Laughs] See Amma? He’s learning.

**Amma:** சரி, Andrew. Next week, we must go to the theater.

**Andrew:** [To Deepa] Is she serious?

**Deepa:** உன் இஷ்டம் (Your choice/preference).

**Andrew:** Uh... சரி, Amma. போகலாம் (Let's go).

**SCENE END**
