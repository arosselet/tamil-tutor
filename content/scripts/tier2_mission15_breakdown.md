# Tier 2 Mission 15: The Arrival (Breakdown)

**Raj:** Next key. தூக்கு. To lift. Or carry.

**Raj:** Simple enough. `ரயில்`. But listen to how they say it. எப்போ ரயில் வரும்? When will the train come?
