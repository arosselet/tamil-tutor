# Script: Level 3, Episode 4 — Possession & Inventory

**Host:** Welcome back. You've navigated the auto and argued over the price of mangoes. But now we need to talk about where things actually are.

**Guest:** Prepositions?

**Host:** In Tamil, they're post-positions. They come *after* the noun. And they are the keys to a tidy life. Or at least, a life where you can find your keys.

**Guest:** Let's hear them.

**Host:** First, the most versatile one. "With" or "Near." Used for people.

**Guest:** கிட்ட.

**Host:** கிட்ட. My phone is "with Raj." Raj-kitta. The auto is "near the shop." Shop-kitta.

**Guest:** கிட்ட.

**Host:** Next, the verticality. On top or Above.

**Guest:** மேல.

**Host:** மேல. Like "Table mela." And the opposite—Below or Under.

**Guest:** கீழ.

**Host:** கீழ. Like "Sofa keela." 

---

**Host:** Now, the logic of containers. Inside.

**Guest:** உள்ள.

**Host:** உள்ள. "Bag ulla." And Outside.

**Guest:** வெளிய.

**Host:** வெளிய. "Auto veliya." 

**Guest:** கிட்ட, மேல, கீழ, உள்ள, வெளிய.

---

**Host:** Let's drill. Your wife is looking for her glasses. You see them on the fridge.

**Guest:** Fridge மேல இருக்கு.

**Host:** Correct. She's looking for the cat. It's under the bed.

**Guest:** Bed கீழ இருக்கு.

**Host:** Your daughter asks for the keys. You have them.

**Guest:** என்கிட்ட இருக்கு.

**Host:** "En-kitta." With me. Perfect. The milk is in the fridge.

**Guest:** Fridge உள்ள இருக்கு.

**Host:** And the delivery guy?

**Guest:** வெளிய இருக்காரா?

**Host:** "Veliya irukkara?" Is he outside? You're adding the respect suffix to the verb now! Semma.

---

**Host:** Boss fight. Your house is a mess. You need to locate five things in ten seconds. Ready?
1. Remote? (On the table)
2. Keys? (In the bag)
3. Shoes? (Outside)
4. Water bottle? (Near the TV)
5. The kids? (Under the table)

**Guest:** Table மேல... இல்ல, Table மேல remote. Bag உள்ள சாவி. வெளிய செருப்பு. TV கிட்ட தண்ணி bottle. Table கீழ பசங்க!

**Host:** A bit frantic, but accurate. You found everything.

---

**Host:** Today's Zinger. The phrase you mutter at every doorway. The locator's mantra.

**Guest:** What is it?

**Host:** எங்க இருக்கு? Where is it? Followed by கிட்ட, மேல, கீழ, உள்ள, வெளிய.

**Guest:** எங்க இருக்கு? கிட்ட, மேல, கீழ, உள்ள, வெளிய.

**Host:** எங்க இருக்கு? கிட்ட, மேல, கீழ, உள்ள, வெளிய.

**Guest:** எங்க இருக்கு? கிட்ட, மேல, கீழ, உள்ள, வெளிய.

**Host:** You've mapped your world. Nothing is lost anymore.
