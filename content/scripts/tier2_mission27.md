# Mission 27: The Favorite Gift (Like & Dislike)

**Cast:**
- **Anna (M):** Elder brother, trying to be patient.
- **Akka (F):** Elder sister, the observant one.
- **Thambi (M):** Younger brother, distraught and distracted.
- **Analyst Maya (F):** Analytical and sharp.
- **Analyst Raj (M):** Enthusiastic and curious.

---

## Part 1: The Intercept

**Location:** Dining Room. The sound of plates clinking. Thambi is sighing.

**Anna (M):** தம்பி, சாப்பாடு நல்லா இருக்கு. ஏன் சாப்பிடல? Just eat a little bit.

**Thambi (M):** எனக்கு பசி இல்ல அண்ணா. I'm not hungry.

**Akka (F):** கவலைப்படாதே தம்பி. Don't be so worried. It's just a box. Look, I made special Biryani today. உனக்கு பிரியாணி பிடிக்கும், இல்லையா? You like Biryani, right?

**Thambi (M):** முன்னாடி பிடிக்கும். I liked it before. இப்போ பிடிக்காது. Now I don't like it.

**Anna (M):** என்னாச்சு உனக்கு? What happened to you? Yesterday you ate two plates! சரி, forget the food. We can watch a movie later. புது விஜய் சினிமா வந்துருக்கு. The new Vijay movie is out. உனக்கு விஜய் சினிமா பிடிக்கும்-னு நினைச்சேன். I thought you liked Vijay movies.

**Thambi (M):** இல்ல அண்ணா. எனக்கு விஜய் சினிமா பிடிக்காது. I don't like Vijay movies. எனக்கு ரஜினி சினிமா தான் பிடிக்கும். I only like Rajini movies.

**Akka (F):** (Sighs) சரி, ரஜினி சினிமா பார்க்கலாம். But தம்பி, listen. Is it really about the box? Was it a gift? அந்த பெட்டி உள்ள ஒரு வாட்ச் இருந்தது, இல்லையா? There was a watch inside that box, right? அந்த வாட்ச் உனக்கு ரொம்ப பிடிக்கும், அதானே? You liked that watch a lot, didn't you?

**Thambi (M):** ஆமா அக்கா. எனக்கு அந்த வாட்ச் ரொம்ப பிடிக்கும். I liked that watch very much. It was my favorite. It's my favorite இஷ்டம்.

**Anna (M):** இஷ்டம்-ஆ? So it was a special preference. Why didn't you say so? Losing it was a big தப்பு. A big mistake.

**Thambi (M):** எனக்கு தெரியும் அண்ணா. I know. அதான் எனக்கு ரொம்ப சோகம். That's why I'm so sad. நான் போறேன். I'm leaving.

**Akka (F):** தம்பி, நில்லு! Wait!

[Sound of Thambi walking away fast]

## Part 2: The Breakdown

**Analyst Maya (F):** Wow, the tension in that dining room is getting worse. Thambi is completely shut down.

**Analyst Raj (M):** Seriously. He's rejecting Biryani! Who rejects Biryani? He literally said இப்போ பிடிக்காது — Now I don't like it.

**Analyst Maya (F):** Right. That's our big engine for today. பிடிக்கும் and பிடிக்காது. Did you hear how he flipped it?

**Analyst Raj (M):** Yeah, it's that classic Tamil pattern we keep seeing. It's not "I like," it's "To me, it is liked." எனக்கு பிடிக்கும்.

**Analyst Maya (F):** Exactly. எனக்கு is "to me," and பிடிக்கும் is "it is liked" or "it pleases." So when Akka asks him about the food, she says:

**Akka (F):** உனக்கு பிரியாணி பிடிக்கும், இல்லையா?

**Analyst Raj (M):** To you, Biryani is liked, right? And Thambi, being a dramatic younger brother, drops the hammer with பிடிக்காது.

**Analyst Maya (F):** That aa sound at the end of பிடிக்காது is the universal sign for "No" or "Negative" in these kinds of verbs. பிடிக்கும் — Yes. பிடிக்காது — No.

**Analyst Raj (M):** He did the same thing with the movies! Anna thought he was a Vijay fan, but Thambi was like, இல்ல அண்ணா. எனக்கு விஜய் சினிமா பிடிக்காது. எனக்கு ரஜினி சினிமா தான் பிடிக்கும்.

**Analyst Maya (F):** I don't like Vijay movies. I only like Rajini movies. He’s very clear about his preferences when he’s upset.

**Analyst Raj (M):** And then there was that other word. இஷ்டம். Thambi said the watch was his favorite இஷ்டம். 

**Analyst Maya (F):** இஷ்டம் is more like a "desire" or a "strong preference." You’ll hear people ask உனக்கு என்ன இஷ்டம்? meaning "What is your wish?" or "What do you prefer?" It’s a bit more personal than just பிடிக்கும்.

**Analyst Raj (M):** It explains why he's so சோகம் — so sad. He really, really liked that watch. 

**Analyst Maya (F):** Poor guy. But we got a huge clue! It wasn't just a "box." It was a gift box with a watch inside. 

**Analyst Raj (M):** And we saw some of our old "struggle" words in action too. Anna called losing it a தப்பு — a mistake. And they keep telling him not to கவலை, not to worry.

**Analyst Maya (F):** But with Thambi, the more you tell him not to worry, the more he goes into his சோகம் mode. 

**Analyst Raj (M):** Well, now we know what we're looking for. A watch. In a box. Somewhere.

**Analyst Maya (F):** எனக்கு அந்த வாட்ச் ரொம்ப பிடிக்கும். He liked it a lot. Let's see if we can find where he "liked" it last.

**Analyst Raj (M):** Before he leaves the house again! பிடிக்கும், பிடிக்காது. Keep those in your head while we track down this watch.

**Analyst Maya (F):** சரி, let's keep moving.
