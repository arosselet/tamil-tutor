# Tier 2, Mission 49 — The Bakery Run

## Core Targets
- **Linguistic Pattern:** The Necessity & Availability (Must do vs Will be available)
- **Scenario Shape:** transaction
- **Location class:** bakery
- **Energy:** medium
- **Episode Form:** classic

## Scenario Context
Andrew is at a local Coimbatore bakery (the famous "KR Bakes" style). He's picking up some snacks for a small family gathering. The atmosphere is bustling—the smell of fresh puffs, the sound of the tea master "pulling" tea. He needs to navigate the ordering process, check if certain things are available, and decide what to take as a parcel.

## Word Payload

**NEW (5 words/phrases):**
- **பார்சல்** (*pār-sal*) — Parcel / Takeaway
- **பணம்** (*paṇ-am*) — Money
- **கிடைக்கும்** (*ki-ḍai-kkum*) — Will be available / Will get
- **வேற ஒன்னும் இல்ல** (*vē-ṟa on-num il-la*) — Nothing else
- **பார்சல் பண்ணுங்க** (*pār-sal paṇ-ṇun-ga*) — Make it a parcel / Pack it for takeaway

**CALLBACKS (5 words):**

- **ஃப்ரீ-யா?** (*p-rī-yā?*) — Are you free? [struggled]
