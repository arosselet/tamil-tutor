## Word Status Sheet

### TEACH (The Keys)
- `கவலை` (Kavalai) - Worry
- `கவலைப்படாதீங்க` (Kavalaipadaadheenga) - Don't worry / Don't be worried. (We will teach the informal too: கவலைப்படாத - Kavalaipadaadha)
- `சோகம்` (Sogam) - Sad / Sadness

### EXPOSE (The Signal - Target Vocab from Tier 2)
- `என்னாச்சு?` (Ennaachu?) - What happened? (A crucial prompt for confession).
- `வெட்கம்` (Vetkam) - Shy / Embarrassment (Used here as shame for losing it).
- `தப்பு` (Thappu) - Mistake / Wrong (Struggled word).
- `உள்ள` (Ulla) - Inside (Mastered word, used for context: "What was inside?").

### USE (Ambient Mastered Words)
- `அண்ணா` (Anna), `தம்பி` (Thambi)
- `எங்க?` (Enga? - Where?)
- `சொல்றேன்` (Solren - I'm saying)
- `பார்த்தேன்` (Paarthen - I saw)
- `பரவாயில்லை` (Paravaayillai - It's okay)

### CALLBACK (Struggled Words to Weave)
- `பெட்டி` (Petti - Box) - Central to the plot.
- `இன்னைக்கு` (Innaikku - Today) - The timeline.
- `முடியாது` (Mudiyaadhu - Cannot) - The helplessness.

## The Interactive Arc

### The Intercept (The Scenario)
**Setting:** The Veranda. Evening. 
**Atmosphere:** Quiet, tense. Not a screaming match like last time, but a defeated confession.

1. **The Setup:** Anna finds Thambi sitting alone. He asks "Ennaachu?" (What happened?).
2. **The Confession:** Thambi says "Anna... that box (petti)... I can't find it." (Kedaikala).
3. **The Interrogation:** Anna asks where (Enga?) he kept it today (Innaikku). Thambi says he doesn't know. 
4. **The Emotion Drop (Payload 1):** Thambi says "Enaku kavalaiya irukku" (I am worried). "Ulla... kaasu irundhudhu." (Inside... there was money). Anna reacts. 
5. **The Emotion Drop 2 (Payload 2):** Thambi says it's a huge mistake (Thappu) and he feels ashamed (Vetkam). He cannot (Mudiyaadhu) face their mother.
6. **The Reassurance (Payload 3):** Anna sighs. He says "Paravaayillai. Kavalaipadaadha. Naan paathukuren." (It's okay. Don't worry. I'll take care of it).

### The Breakdown (The Analysis)
1. **The Teaser:** "Someone just confessed to losing something very valuable. Listen for the word that means 'worry'. It's the key to the whole scene."
2. **The Replay Hook:** "Let's go back to that moment: Thambi says *'Anna... Enaku kavalaiya irukku'*."
3. **The Drill (Kavalai):** Break down *Kavalai* (Worry). Note how it pairs with the Dative pattern they just learned in Mission 20/25 (Enaku kavalaiya irukku - To me, it is worrisome).
4. **The Antidote (Don't Worry):** If *Kavalai* is the problem, the solution is *Kavalaipadaadheenga* (Don't worry). Host and guest practice the formal and informal (Kavalaipadaadha) versions, as Anna used it.
5. **The Weight (Sogam & Vetkam):** Unpack Thambi's other emotions. He's not just worried; he made a *Thappu* (Mistake) and feels *Vetkam* (Shame/Embarrassment). Note how *Vetkam* shifts from "shy" to "embarrassed" based on context.
6. **The Wrap:** Recap the stakes. The box had money inside. Anna told him not to worry. But the open loop remains—how will they fix this?
