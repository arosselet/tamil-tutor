# Tier 2 Mission 15: The Arrival (Briefing)

## Location: Coimbatore Railway Station
**Objective:** Eavesdrop on a passenger and a porter to recover Intel about a missing "Black Bag."

## Word Status Sheet

- **TEACH (The Keys):** 
  - தூக்கு (Lift / Carry)
  - நிமிஷம் (Minute)
  - ரயில் (Train)
  - பெட்டி (Box / Suitcase)
- **EXPOSE (The Signal):** 
  - டிக்கெட் (Ticket)
  - பாரம் (Heavy / Burden)
  - சீக்கிரம் (Quickly)
  - எப்போ (When)
  - அங்க (There)
- **USE:** வணக்கம், அண்ணா, பணம், இல்ல, சரி.
- **CALLBACK:** சொன்னேன் (I said), குடு (Give).

## The Narrative Arc

### Track A: The Intercept (The Eavesdrop)
- **Scenario:** A Passenger is frantic. A Porter (Coolie) is trying to handle a heavy trunk.
- **Dialogue Highlights:** 
  - Passenger asking when the train arrives.
  - Porter telling him to lift the box quickly.
  - Mention of a "Black Box" (கருப்பு பெட்டி) left over there.

### Track B: The Breakdown (Analytic Decoding)
- **Analysts:** Analyst Maya & Analyst Raj.
- **Focus:** 
  - Decoding the verb 'தூக்கு' (Lift) vs 'வை' (Keep).
  - The use of 'நிமிஷம்' (Minute) in time pressure.
  - Replaying the "Missing Bag" clue.
