# Tier 2 Mission 13: The Stream (Say & Ask Remix)

## Word Status Sheet

- **TEACH:** சொல்றேன் (I'm saying), சொல்லுவேன் (I will say), கேட்டேன் (I asked), கேக்குறேன் (I'm asking), கேட்பேன் (I will ask)
- **USE:** சொன்னேன் (I said), அண்ணா (elder brother), அக்கா (elder sister), அவங்க (respectful third person), ஆமா (yes), இல்ல (no), சரி (okay), வணக்கம் (hello)
- **CALLBACK:** இன்னைக்கு (Today), முதல்ல (First), முன்னாடி (Before), அப்புறம் (Later/After)

## The Angle & Allocation

- **Vibe:** "The Stream / Low Energy Remix". This is a chill, hypnotic, repetitive exercise designed for an evening walk or relaxing on the couch. No pressure scenarios, just rhythmic pacing.
- **Goal:** Burn the Present and Future tense of "Say" and "Ask" into muscle memory by creating a rhythmic "Matrix" drill alongside the Past tense anchor ("I said").

## The Beat Sheet

### 1. The Wind Down (Setting the Chill Vibe)
- **Beat 1: Intro:** Host sets a low-energy, evening walk vibe. Instructs Guest (learner) to drop shoulders and relax.
- **Beat 2: Anchor Concept:** Host reminds Guest of their anchor word from earlier today: "I said" (`USE`: சொன்னேன்).
- **Beat 3: Connection:** Host explains the `-en` ending as the anchor for "I" in the past, setting the stage to build the timeline around it.

### 2. The Slow Flow (Say - Present)
- **Beat 4: Concept (Present):** Bring mind to the present. "I am saying" (`TEACH`: சொல்றேன்). Notice the shift to `-reen`.
- **Beat 5: Application:** Say it to Akka now. "I am telling Akka" (அக்கா கிட்ட சொல்றேன்).
- **Beat 6: Callback Drill:** Use callback "Today". "Today, I am saying" (இன்னைக்கு சொல்றேன்).
- **Beat 7: Contrast:** Past vs Present. I said, I am saying. (சொன்னேன், சொல்றேன்).

### 3. The Horizon (Say - Future)
- **Beat 8: Concept (Future):** Look ahead. "I will say" (`TEACH`: சொல்லுவேன்). The `-veen` ending reaches forward.
- **Beat 9: Application:** Say it to Anna tomorrow. "I will tell Anna" (அண்ணா கிட்ட சொல்லுவேன்).
- **Beat 10: Callback Drill:** Sequence with callback "First". "First I said, then I will say" (முதல்ல சொன்னேன், அப்புறம் சொல்லுவேன்).

### 4. The Say Matrix Drill
- **Beat 11: Rhythm Setup:** Walking through the museum of time. Past, Present, Future.
- **Beat 12: Repetition 1:** I said (சொன்னேன்).
- **Beat 13: Repetition 2:** I am saying (சொல்றேன்).
- **Beat 14: Repetition 3:** I will say (சொல்லுவேன்).
- **Beat 15: The Cycle:** Host guides Guest through the cycle. Past. Present. Future. (சொன்னேன். சொல்றேன். சொல்லுவேன்.)

### 5. The Pivot (Ask - Past)
- **Beat 16: Concept (Swap):** Keep the walking rhythm, swap "Say" for "Ask". Frame is the same.
- **Beat 17: Application:** "I asked" (`TEACH`: கேட்டேன்). Uses the exact same `-en` anchor as I said.
- **Beat 18: Banter:** I asked Anna (அண்ணா கிட்ட கேட்டேன்).

### 6. The Ask Flow (Ask - Present)
- **Beat 19: Concept (Present Question):** Bring it to now. "I am asking" (`TEACH`: கேக்குறேன்). Hearing the `-reen` ending again.
- **Beat 20: Application:** "I am asking you" (உங்க கிட்ட கேக்குறேன்).

### 7. The Future Question (Ask - Future)
- **Beat 21: Concept (Future Question):** Look to the future. "I will ask" (`TEACH`: கேட்பேன்). The `-peen` ending.
- **Beat 22: Application:** I will ask Anna (அண்ணா கிட்ட கேட்பேன்).
- **Beat 23: Callback Drill:** Use callback "Before". "Before I asked. Later I will ask." (முன்னாடி கேட்டேன். அப்புறம் கேட்பேன்).

### 8. The Full Wash (The Remix Matrix)
- **Beat 24: Concept (Dual Engine):** Run both engines side by side.
- **Beat 25: Past Drill:** I said. I asked. (சொன்னேன். கேட்டேன்.)
- **Beat 26: Present Drill:** I am saying. I am asking. (சொல்றேன். கேக்குறேன்.)
- **Beat 27: Future Drill:** I will say. I will ask. (சொல்லுவேன். கேட்பேன்.)
- **Beat 28: Weaving People Part 1:** I told Akka. I asked Anna.
- **Beat 29: Weaving People Part 2:** I am telling Anna. I am asking Akka. 
- **Beat 30: Final Sweep:** Past. Present. Future. Let the sounds wash over.

### 9. The Quiet Outro & Zinger
- **Beat 31: Zinger Intro:** Host provides the quiet Zinger for ending a conversation.
- **Beat 32: Zinger Delivery:** "I will say it later/I will tell you later" (நான் அப்புறம் சொல்லுவேன்).
- **Beat 33: Outro:** Host tells the Learner to mutter the Zinger into the night air, trust the memory, and enjoy the rest of the walk. "சரி, போயிட்டு வரேன்."
