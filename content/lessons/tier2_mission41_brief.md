# Mission 41 Brief: The Degree Coffee Ritual

**Scene:** The Kitchen Command Center.
**Stakes:** Akka (Elder Sister) is making her legendary "Degree Coffee." The learner (Host B) is the "assistant" who is currently failing to meet her exacting standards.
**Energy:** High friction, impatient, domestic chaos.

**Word Payload:**
- **சமை (Samai)** — To cook / Prepare.
- **ஊத்து (Oothu)** — To pour.
- **கலக்கு (Kalakku)** — To mix / Stir.
- **திற (Thira)** — To open.
- **மூடு (Moodu)** — To close / Shut.
- **கழுவு (Kazhuvu)** — To wash (dishes/hands).

**Callbacks:**
- **வை (Vai)** — Put / Place / Keep.
- **தூக்கு (Thookku)** — Lift / Carry.
- **சாப்டீங்களா? (Saaptingala?)** — Did you eat?
- **கேட்குறேன் (Ketkuren)** — I am asking.

**Director's Notes for Architect:**
- Start with the sound of a boiling milk pot.
- Akka should be barking orders. "Open this!", "Pour that!", "Don't stir like a robot!"
- Host B (Learner) should sound slightly overwhelmed but trying.
- The transition to the Breakdown should feel like Maya and Raj are "decompressing" from the stress of the kitchen.
