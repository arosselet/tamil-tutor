# Tier 2, Mission 47 — The Weekend Plan

## Scene Seed
Host B is in the middle of cleaning his room, physically moving heavy storage boxes (**தூக்கு** and **வை**). Host A calls him to coordinate a movie plan with a group of friends for the weekend. Host B is distracted and groggy, but has to manage the logistical questions.

## Word Payload
- **NEW (Scheduling):**
  - **ஃப்ரீ-யா?** (Are you free?)
  - **நேரம் இருக்கா?** (Do you have time?)
  - **கேன்சல்** (Cancel)
  - **போஸ்ட்போன்** (Postpone)
- **CALLBACKS (Struggles):**
  - **கேட்குறேன்** (I'm asking - present)
  - **கேட்பேன்** (I will ask - future)
  - **தூக்கு** (Lift)
  - **வை** (Keep/Place)
  - **நிஜமா?** (Really?)

## Interaction Beats
1. **The Physical Struggle:** Host B is audible struggling. Host A asks what's happening. B says he has to **தூக்கு** (lift) these boxes and **வை** (place) them on the shelf.
2. **The Ask:** Host A asks "நாளைக்கு **ஃப்ரீ-யா?**" (Are you free tomorrow?). B says "இப்போ **நேரம் இல்ல**" (No time now). Host A pushes: "**நேரம் இருக்கா?**" (Do you have time later?).
3. **The Logistical Loop:** Host A asks if B talked to Rahul. B says "**கேட்குறேன்**" (I am asking him now). A says "No, ask him about the movie." B says "சரி, அப்புறம் **கேட்பேன்**" (Okay, I will ask later).
4. **The Crisis:** Host A says if everyone is busy, we have to **கேன்சல்** (cancel). B says "**நிஜமா?**" (Really?). He suggests we just **போஸ்ட்போன்** (postpone) to Sunday instead.

## Calibration Notes
- Emphasize the **கேட்குறேன்** (present) vs **கேட்பேன்** (future) distinction in the Analysts' breakdown.
- Ensure the physical effort of **தூக்கு** is audible in the Intercept to anchor the verb.

## Target Duration
- 5-7 minutes.
