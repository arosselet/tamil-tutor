# Tier 2, Mission 53 — The Kitchen Call

## Core Targets
- **Linguistic Pattern:** The Present-Action Question — "என்ன ___ கிற?" — probing what someone is doing right now. In this episode it functions both literally (what food is cooking?) and as a weapon of suspicion (what are you hiding?).
- **Register:** suspicion (from Scene Spec)
- **Dramatic Ingredient:** stakes — the maama who "wasn't supposed to be there" is revealed by a phrase, not an accusation
- **Scenario Shape:** eavesdrop (we overhear both sides of a private phone call)
- **Location class:** kitchen
- **Energy:** medium
- **Episode Form:** phone_call (light Breakdown)

## Scenario Context

Post-wedding-hall break. Host A told Host B she was exhausted and going straight home to rest. Host B calls to check in — and immediately hears it: vessels clinking, water running, the unmistakable sounds of someone mid-cook. He doesn't accuse. He just asks: "என்ன சமைக்கிற?" She deflects. He asks again. The question isn't about the food. When she finally admits who's there, Host B predicts the next line before it lands — "சுவையா இருக்குன்னு சொல்வாரு இப்போ" — and Host A confirms with quiet defeat: "...சுவையா இருக்கு." Then, a beat later: "சாப்பாடு ரெடி ஆயிடுது." The jig is completely up. The call ends with Host B's resigned "சாப்பிட்டிட்டு போ."

The stakes: she said she was done with the maama thread. The kitchen said otherwise.

## Word Payload

**NEW (4 phrases — soak order, all four now in lexicon):**
- **சமையலறை** (*samayalarai*) — kitchen. Appears early: Host B's first suspicious probe "சமையலறைல?" frames the word in pure context.
- **என்ன சமைக்கிற?** (*enna samaikkira*) — what are you cooking? FOCAL PHRASE: 6 answering-context appearances across Intercept + Breakdown. Knocked on today; came back hinted (English-instinct reflex) — maximum repetition, each with immediate context.
- **சுவையா இருக்கு** (*suvaiya irukku*) — it's tasty. Lands in two-beat dramatic sequence: Host B predicts it, Host A confirms it — each exposure answers the phrase's meaning through the emotional charge of the moment.
- **சாப்பாடு ரெடி** (*saappaadu ready*) — food is ready. The admission that ends the call. Appears in Intercept as the surrender line, then twice more in Breakdown (quoted and reprised as closing joke).

**CALLBACKS (2 words — soft targets, both fit naturally):**
- **சீக்கிரம்** (*seekkiram*) — Quickly [floor-gap] — Host B's dry "சீக்கிரம் ready ஆகுமா?" edges the suspicion without confronting it directly.
- **சொன்னாங்க** (*sonnaangka*) — They said [floor-gap] — Host A's deflection: "யாரு சமைக்கிறேன்னு சொன்னாங்க?" (who said I was cooking?).
