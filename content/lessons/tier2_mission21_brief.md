# Mission Brief: Mission 21 - The Worrying Aunt

**Location:** The Family House — Coimbatore
**Intel Target:** Determine why the Aunt is worried and understand the emotions she is expressing.
**Narrative Style:** The Heated Dispute / Family Drama (Focus on emotions)

## Narrative Arc (Intercept)
1. Aunt asks where Anna is, noting it's late.
2. Agent doesn't know, asks what happened.
3. Aunt expresses her worry and fear.
4. Agent tries to comfort her.
5. Aunt insists he calls Anna first, mentioning she feels sad today.
6. Agent calms her down, offers water, and tells her not to worry.

## Narrative Arc (Breakdown)
1. **Cold Open Teaser:** Plant the target: "Listen for the word that means 'worry'. The Aunt uses it to describe how she's feeling right now."
2. **Replay Hook:** Go straight to the core moment "எனக்கு கவலை" and translate it.
3. **The Full Unpacking:** Step through every single line of the Intercept. Translate everything so the listener has 100% comprehension of the scene.
4. **Key Deep Dive:** Explain the Dative Emotion pattern (எனக்கு + emotion) using 'கவலை', 'பயம்', and 'சோகம்'.
5. **Drill Beats:** Prompt the user to produce the words for worry, fear, and sadness.
6. **Closing:** Wrap up with the callback word 'முதல்ல' seamlessly.
