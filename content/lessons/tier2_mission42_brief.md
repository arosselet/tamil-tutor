# MISSION BRIEF: Tier 2, Mission 42

**BEAT:** A busy Coimbatore tea stall. A customer is convinced she's been short-changed. The vendor is equally convinced he's right. The dispute escalates until the missing note turns up under the counter tray. Something changes: the vendor is proven wrong, the customer is vindicated, and the transaction closes.

**NEW:**
- **கொடுத்தேன்** (*kodutten*) — I gave
- **வந்தேன்** (*vanden*) — I came
- **அதான்** (*adaan*) — That's why / Exactly / See, I told you
- **புரிஞ்சுது** (*purinjudhu*) — Got it / I understood
- **புரியல** (*puriyala*) — I don't understand
