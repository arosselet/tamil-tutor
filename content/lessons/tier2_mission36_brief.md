=== MISSION BRIEF: Tier 2, Mission 36 ===

SEED:
A Coimbatore auto stand. Two strangers — Senthil and Meena — are both going to different but nearby stops and attempt to share the fare. They spend the entire ride arguing over whose stop comes first and which way to turn. The auto driver Murugan just wants someone, anyone, to make a decision.

LISTENABILITY:
The shared-auto negotiation is a universal urban comedy. Nobody is wrong, nobody is right, and the person least interested in the argument is the one driving. The speed bump at the end is inevitable.

  - Mission 34: Mechanic Workshop. No repairs, no bike problems, no tools, no "late for work" drama.
  - Mission 33: Delivery Driver. No deliveries, no apps, no waiting outside eateries.
  - Avoid: Phones, market vendors, and the "Andrew" character.
