# Mission 28 Brief: The Emotional Debrief (BOSS FIGHT)

- **Location:** The Family House — Dining Room (Lunchtime)
- **Style:** The Field Test / The Emotional Debrief
- **Narrative Context Update:**
    - **Current Arc:** The Missing Box Incident
    - **Last Event:** Mama tells a dramatic story about the neighbor Suresh's misfortune to distract Thambi, using it as a "teaching moment" for emotions.
    - **Open Loop:** Thambi's reaction to the story might reveal if his "missing box" contains something related to these emotions.
- **Intel Target:** Identify and map 4 distinct emotional shifts in Mama's story (Happy -> Fear -> Angry -> Worry).

    - **கோவம்** (Kovam) — Angry / Anger

- **EXPOSE (The Signal):**
    - **பெருமை** (Perumai) — Proud
    - **கவலை** (Kavalai) — Worry / Worried
    - **சோகம்** (Sogam) — Sad
    - **பிடிக்கும்** (Pidikkum) — Like
    - **பிடிக்காது** (Pidikkadhu) — Dislike
    - **இஷ்டம்** (Ishtam) — Preference / Liking
    - **வெட்கம்** (Vetkam) — Shy / Embarrassed
- **USE (Mastered):**
    - **தம்பி** (Thambi), **மாமா** (Mama), **அண்ணா** (Anna), **அக்கா** (Akka), **செம்ம** (Semma), **சரி** (Sari), **என்ன** (Enna), **எங்க** (Enga).
- **CALLBACK (Struggled):**
    - **பெட்டி** (Petti) — Box (The central mystery object)
    - **கீழ** (Keezha) — Under (Where was the box?)
    - **முதல்ல** (Mudhalla) — First (The start of the story)
    - **அப்புறம்** (Appuram) — After/Then (The sequence)

## The Narrative Arc

### The Intercept (The Scenario)
1. **The Setup:** Thambi is still pouting about his missing **பெட்டி** (box). Mama sits down with his coffee and says, "Forget the box for a minute, did you hear about Suresh?"
2. **Beat 1 (Happy/Proud):** Mama describes how Suresh **முதல்ல** (first) got a huge job in Chennai. Everyone was so **சந்தோஷம்** (happy). His father felt **பெருமை** (proud).
3. **Beat 2 (Fear):** But then, the landlord came. He said Suresh can't leave. Suresh felt big **பயம்** (fear). He hid his money **கீழ** (under) the bed.
4. **Beat 3 (Angry):** Suresh's brother found the money and took it to buy a bike! Suresh was so **கோவம்** (angry). He shouted at everyone.
5. **Beat 4 (Worry/Sad):** Now the mother is sitting alone, she has so much **கவலை** (worry). The house is just **சோகம்** (sadness). 
6. **The Twist:** Mama asks Thambi, "So, in your **பெட்டி**, was it something that makes you **சந்தோஷம்** or something that gives you **பயம்**?" Thambi looks down, silent.

### The Breakdown (The Analysis)
1. **Cold Open Teaser:** "You're about to hear Mama weave a neighborhood soap opera. Listen for the word **சந்தோஷம்**. It's the spark that starts the whole drama."
2. **Replay Hook:** "Let's go back to when Suresh got the job... [Quote: 'Ellarukkum semma sandhosham!']. Did you feel the energy shift?"
3. **Key 1: The Dative Emotion:** Explain why it's "Enakku sandhosham" (To me, happiness) instead of "I am happy."
4. **Key 2: The Anger/Fear Toggle:** Contrast **கோவம்** and **பயம்**. How the landlord brings one, and the brother brings the other.
5. **Key 3: The Worry/Sadness Spectrum:** Unpack **கவலை** (active worry) vs **சோகம்** (heavy sadness).
6. **The "Boss" Challenge:** Recapping the 4 beats. Can you name the emotion for each person in the story?
7. **Narrative Outro:** Back to the Dining Room. Thambi's silence is heavy. We need to find that box.
