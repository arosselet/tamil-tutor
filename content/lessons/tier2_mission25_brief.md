# Mission Brief: Tier 2, Mission 25 — The Dative Emotion Pattern

## Metadata
- **Location:** The Family House — Dining Room (Lunchtime)
- **Intel Target:** Decoding the `Enakku` (To me) dative pattern for expressing emotions.
- **Narrative Style:** The Heated Dispute / Family Negotiation
- **Characters:** **Mama (M)**, **Akka (F)**, **Thambi (M)**

## Word Status Sheet

### TEACH (The Keys)
- **எனக்கு சந்தோஷம்** → I am happy (lit: To me, joy)
- **எனக்கு பயமா இருக்கு** → I am scared (lit: To me, it is fearful)
- **எனக்கு கவலையா இருக்கு** → I am worried (lit: To me, it is worrisome)

### EXPOSE (The Signal)
- **கோவம்** → Angry
- **கல்யாணம்** → Wedding
- **பிடிக்கும்** → I like
- **பிடிக்காது** → I don't like
- **நிஜமா?** → Really?

### USE (Mastered/Comfortable)
- **சாப்பிட்டேன்** → I ate
- **சாப்பிடுங்க** → Eat (respectful)
- **சரி** → Okay
- **ஆமா** → Yes
- **இல்ல** → No
- **என்னாச்சு?** → What happened?

### CALLBACK (Struggled)
- **தப்பு** → Wrong/Mistake
- **முன்னாடி** → Before
- **வை** → Keep/Put
- **நிமிஷம்** → Minute

## Narrative Arc

### The Intercept (The Scenario)
1. **The Setting:** Clatter of plates. Mama is picking at his food, looking stressed. Thambi is teasing him.
2. **The Conflict:** Mama mentions the upcoming **கல்யாணம்** (wedding) in the family. He says he has "Budget tension."
3. **The Reveal:** Akka enters, beaming. She’s heard the groom’s family is great. She says **எனக்கு சந்தோஷம்** (I am happy).
4. **The Friction:** Mama snaps back. He’s the one paying. **எனக்கு கவலையா இருக்கு** (I am worried).
5. **The Prank:** Thambi tries to lighten the mood by telling a fake story about a ghost in the wedding hall. Akka, who is superstitious, says **எனக்கு பயமா இருக்கு** (I am scared).
6. **The Correction:** Mama tells Thambi it's **தப்பு** (wrong) to joke like that. "Keep the plate there" (**வை**). 
7. **The Exit:** They agree to talk in a **நிமிஷம்** (minute) after finishing lunch.

### The Breakdown (The Analysis)
1. **Cold Open Teaser:** *"You're about to hear a family lunch turn into a debate about a wedding. Listen for the word 'Enakku'. It's the secret code that turns a simple emotion into a natural Tamil sentence."*
2. **Replay Hook:** *"Let's go back to the moment Akka walked in. She was glowing. She didn't say 'I am happy.' She said... [எனக்கு சந்தோஷம்]."*
3. **The Dative Logic:** Maya and Raj unpack the "To me" concept. Why we don't say "I am" but "To me, it is."
4. **The 'Irukku' Toggle:** Explaining why **கவலை** (worry) and **பயம்** (fear) often take the "is/exists" (**இருக்கு**) ending while **சந்தோஷம்** can stand alone or take it.
5. **The "Bayam" Replay:** Replaying Thambi's ghost joke and Akka's reaction.
6. **The Like/Dislike Bridge:** Quickly showing how **பிடிக்கும்** and **பிடிக்காது** use the same **எனக்கு** pattern.
7. **Closing Banter:** Raj tries to use the pattern to express his "happiness" about the lunch, Maya teases him.
