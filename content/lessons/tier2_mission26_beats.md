# Mission 21 Beats: The Missing Box (Worry & Sadness)

## Strategic Outline
- **Location:** The Family House (Veranda)
- **Style:** The Confession (Low energy, high tension)
- **TEACH:** கவலை (Worry), கவலைப்படாதீங்க / கவலைப்படாத (Don't worry), சோகம் (Sadness)
- **EXPOSE:** என்னாச்சு? (What happened?), வெட்கம் (Ashamed/Shy), தப்பு (Mistake), உள்ள (Inside)
- **CALLBACK:** இன்னைக்கு (Today), பெட்டி (Box), முடியாது (Cannot)
- **USE:** எங்க? (Where?), பார்த்தேன் (I saw), சொல்றேன் (I'm saying), அண்ணா (Elder brother), தம்பி (Younger brother), பரவாயில்லை (It's okay).

## The Intercept (The Scenario)
- **Beat 1: The Setup:** Veranda. Crickets. Anna finds Thambi sitting in the dark. Asks "என்னாச்சு?" (What happened?).
- **Beat 2: The Confession:** Thambi is hesitant. Says "அண்ணா... நான் சொல்றேன்... ஆனா கோவப்படாதீங்க." (Anna... I'm telling you... but don't get angry). He mentions the "பெட்டி" (Box).
- **Beat 3: The Interrogation:** Anna asks "பெட்டி எங்க?" (Where is the box?). Thambi says "இன்னைக்கு காபி ஷாப் போனேன்..." (Today I went to the coffee shop...). He can't find it.
- **Beat 4: The Emotion Drop (Payload 1):** Thambi confesses the contents. "உள்ள காசு இருக்கு." (Inside there is money). He says "எனக்கு ரொம்ப கவலையா இருக்கு." (I am very worried). 
- **Beat 5: The Emotion Drop 2 (Payload 2):** Thambi adds, "இது பெரிய தப்பு." (This is a big mistake). "எனக்கு வெட்கமா இருக்கு." (I feel ashamed). "அம்மா கிட்ட சொல்ல முடியாது." (I cannot tell Mom). 
- **Beat 6: The Reassurance:** Silence from Anna. Then a sigh. "பரவாயில்லை." (It's okay). "கவலைப்படாத." (Don't worry). "நான் பாத்துக்கறேன்." (I'll take care of it).

## The Breakdown (The Reactions)
- **Beat 7: The Cold Open Teaser:** Maya drops the hook: *Someone just confessed to losing something very valuable. Listen for the word that means 'worry'. It's the key to the whole scene.*
- **Beat 8: The Replay Hook:** Raj pulls the pivotal quote: *"Let's go back to that moment: Thambi says 'எனக்கு ரொம்ப கவலையா இருக்கு'."*
- **Beat 9: Unpacking Kavalai:** Drill into *கவலை* (Worry). Note the Dative construction (எனக்கு கவலையா இருக்கு - To me, it is worrisome) from Mission 20/25.
- **Beat 10: The Antidote:** Maya points out Anna's response: *கவலைப்படாத* (Don't worry). Contrast the formal (கவலைப்படாதீங்க) with Thambi's informal usage.
- **Beat 11: Unpacking Vetkam & Thappu:** Raj translates the rest of the confession. He made a *தப்பு* (Mistake). He feels *வெட்கம்*. Maya highlights that *Vetkam* usually means "shy" in a romantic/cute context, but here it means "ashamed" because he messed up.
- **Beat 12: Translating the Logistics:** Raj pieces together the rest. The *பெட்டி* (box). The money *உள்ள* (inside). The fact he *முடியாது* (cannot) tell mom.
- **Beat 13: Summary:** Maya recaps. He's worried (*கவலை*), ashamed (*வெட்கம்*), because of a mistake (*தப்பு*). Anna said don't worry (*கவலைப்படாத*). The scene ends on a cliffhanger: they still have to find the box.
