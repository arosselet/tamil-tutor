# Mission 30 Brief: The Tall Conductor (Comparison & Difficulty)

**Location:** Coimbatore — Busy Bus Stop (Gandhipuram direction)
**Narrative Context Update:**
- **Current Arc:** The Coimbatore Return
- **Last Event:** Andrew left the house to run errands. He's now at a major bus intersection trying to figure out the local transit.
- **Open Loop:** Can Andrew navigate the "Change only" rule of Coimbatore private buses?

**Intel Target:** Use comparison to identify the right person/bus and navigate a difficult transaction.

## Word Status Sheet

- **TEACH (The Keys):**
  - **விட** (*Vida*) - Than (Comparison marker)
  - **உயரம்** (*Uyaram*) - Tall / Height
  - **கஷ்டம்** (*Kashtam*) - Difficult / Hardship

- **EXPOSE (The Signal):**
  - **டிக்கெட்** (Ticket) - Loan word anchor
  - **சில்லறை** (*Sillarai*) - Change / Coins
  - **முன்னாடி** (*Munnadi*) - In front / Before
  - **பஸ்** (Bus) - Anchor

- **USE (Mastered):**
  - **எங்க?** (Where?)
  - **போகணும்** (Must go)
  - **முடியும் / முடியாது** (Can / Cannot)
  - **எவ்ளோ?** (How much?)

- **CALLBACK (Struggled):**
  - **நிமிஷம்** (Minute) - Time pressure
  - **லேட் ஆச்சு** (It's late) - Urgency
  - **சரியான** (Correct/Right) - Validation

## Style: The Transaction / The Eavesdrop
The scene is high energy. Traffic noise, horns, and the shouting of bus conductors. Andrew is trying to get on a bus to Gandhipuram but is caught in the chaos of a queue dispute.

## Narrative Arc
1. **The Chaos:** Andrew is at the stop. He's worried because "Late aachu" (It's late).
2. **The Eavesdrop:** Two men are arguing about who was in line first. Man A says, "I was here **munnadi** (before)!" Man B says, "But I am **uyaram** (taller) **vida** (than) you, I can see the bus coming first!" (A nonsense argument to show height comparison).
3. **The Bus:** The Gandhipuram bus screams to a halt. The Conductor leans out.
4. **The Transaction:** Andrew asks "Gandhipuram poganum?" (Must go to Gandhipuram?). The Conductor says "Ticket pathu rooba" (Ticket is 10 rupees).
5. **The Conflict:** Andrew hands over a 500 rupee note. The Conductor yells about "Sillarai" (Change). 
6. **The Resolution:** Andrew says it's "Romba kashtam" (Very difficult) to find change. A passenger helps him out. Andrew gets on, but the bus is packed.
