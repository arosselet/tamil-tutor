# Mission 27 Brief: The Favorite Gift (Like & Dislike)

**Location:** The Family House — Dining Room (Lunchtime)
**Narrative Context Update:**
- **Current Arc:** The Missing Box Incident
- **Last Event:** Thambi is visibly upset; we try to distract him by talking about things he likes to find out what was in the box.
- **Open Loop:** What exactly was in the box that Thambi "liked" so much?

**Intel Target:** Decode Thambi's preferences to identify the contents of the missing box.

## Word Status Sheet

- **TEACH (The Keys):**
  - **பிடிக்கும்** (*Piddikkum*) - I like / It pleases
  - **பிடிக்காது** (*Piddikkaadhu*) - I don't like
  - **இஷ்டம்** (*Ishtam*) - Preference / Liking

- **EXPOSE (The Signal):**
  - **எனக்கு பிடிக்கும்** (*Enakku piddikkum*) - I like it (To me, it is liked)
  - **பிரியாணி** (Biryani) - Noun anchor
  - **சினிமா** (Cinema/Movies) - Noun anchor
  - **ரஜினி** (Rajini) - Cultural anchor (Superstar)
  - **விஜய்** (Vijay) - Cultural anchor (Actor)

- **USE (Mastered):**
  - **சாப்பிட்டேன்** (I ate)
  - **பார்த்தேன்** (I saw)
  - **அண்ணா** (Anna)
  - **தம்பி** (Thambi)

- **CALLBACK (Struggled):**
  - **கவலை** (Worry) - Thambi's current state.
  - **சோகம்** (Sadness) - The atmosphere.
  - **தப்பு** (Wrong/Mistake) - Discussing if losing the box was a "wrong" thing.

## Style: The Eavesdrop / Conversation
We are sitting at the table. Thambi is picking at his food. Anna and Akka are trying to cheer him up by talking about his favorite things, hoping to trigger a memory or a clue about the box.

## Narrative Arc: Intercept (Discovery)
1. **The Gloom:** Thambi pushes his plate away. Anna asks why he isn't eating.
2. **The Distraction:** Akka mentions a new movie. "You like Vijay movies, right?"
3. **The Preference:** Thambi says "I don't like Vijay movies anymore, I like Rajini movies."
4. **The Pivot:** Anna asks about food. "I made Biryani because you like it."
5. **The Clue:** Akka asks, "What about that gift? You liked that watch in the box, right?"
6. **The Reaction:** Thambi gets quiet. "I liked it a lot... but it's gone."
7. **The End:** Thambi leaves the table, even more sad.

## Narrative Arc: Breakdown (Decryption)
1. **The Cold Open Teaser:** "You're about to hear a family lunch turn into a game of 'What do you like?' Listen for the word **Piddikkum**. It's the engine of this whole scene."
2. **The Replay Hook:** "Let's go back to when Akka asks about the movie. She says *Vijay cinema piddikkuma?* and Thambi says *Piddikkaadhu*. Did you hear that hard 'aa' sound at the end? That's the 'No'."
3. **Analysis - The Engine:** Explain the dative pattern: *Enakku piddikkum* (To me, it is liked).
4. **Analysis - The Negative:** Contrast *Piddikkum* vs *Piddikkaadhu*.
5. **Analysis - The Preference:** Introduce *Ishtam* as a slightly more formal/specific preference word used in the conversation.
6. **The Callback Check:** Quickly touch on Thambi's **Kavalai** and why losing the box wasn't his **Thappu**.
7. **The Mission Success:** Summarize the clue—the box contained a watch he liked.
