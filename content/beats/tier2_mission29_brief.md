# Mission Brief: Tier 2, Mission 29 — The Audit Under the Table

**Location:** The Family House — Dining Room (Post-Lunch)
**Mission Style:** The Surveillance / Field Test (Audit Day)

## Narrative Context Update
- **Current Arc:** The Missing Box Incident
- **Last Event:** Andrew has finished the emotional debrief with Thambi. Thambi admits he is 'scared' (பயம்) about the box.
- **Open Loop:** Andrew begins a systematic search of the dining room. Thambi is 'helping' but seems to be guiding Andrew away from certain spots.

## Intel Target
Andrew must decode the spatial directions Thambi is giving to find where the box *isn't*, which will lead him to where it *is*.

## The Narrative Arc

### The Intercept (The Scenario)
1. Andrew starts moving chairs. Thambi is anxious.
2. Andrew asks: "Box is here?" (பெட்டி இங்க இருக்கா?).
3. Thambi says: "No, not here. Look under the table." (இல்ல, இங்க இல்ல. டேபிள் கீழ பாருங்க).
4. Andrew looks under (கீழ). Nothing. Thambi says "Look up/above the fridge." (பிரிட்ஜ் மேல பாருங்க).
5. Andrew checks above (மேல). Just dust.
6. Thambi gets confused: "First look there, then look here." (முதல்ல அங்க பாருங்க, அப்புறம் இங்க பாருங்க).
7. Andrew realizes Thambi is using 'not' patterns. "I can't find it!" (என்னால கண்டுபிடிக்க முடியாது!).
8. Andrew finds a small 'wrong' (தப்பு) clue—a toy piece near the curtain.

### The Breakdown (The Analysis)
1. **The Cold Open Teaser:** "You're about to hear Andrew and Thambi tearing apart the dining room. Listen for the word that means 'under'—it's where the search starts, but not where it ends."
2. **The Replay Hook:** Replay the moment Thambi says "டேபிள் கீழ பாருங்க" (Look under the table). "Did you catch that 'Keela'? It's the most important direction you'll hear today."
3. **The Spatial Anchors:** Unpack `கீழ` (Down) and `மேல` (Up). Use the 'Fridge' and 'Table' as anchors.
4. **The Object Key:** Deep dive into `பெட்டி` (Box). Why this word is driving the whole arc.
5. **The Logic of 'First/Then':** Break down `முதல்ல` (First) and `அப்புறம்` (Afterwards) as the glue for the search sequence.
6. **The Negative Twist:** Explain `இல்ல` (Not) and `முடியாது` (Cannot). How to say "It's not here" vs "I can't".
7. **The Emotional Callback:** Briefly touch on why Thambi is still acting out of `பயம்` (fear) even during the search.
