MISSION BRIEF: Tier 2, Mission 38

BEAT: You're in the living room with Akka (elder sister) after a big family wedding. She's spilling the beans on the 'Murai Maman' (potential marriage match) situation and some gossip about Mama's new house. You have to track who said what and use 'Gossip Tags' to react naturally. The tension is high as Akka reveals a secret about who really asked for the wedding to happen first.

NEW:
  - கல்யாணம் (*kalyanam*) — Wedding
  - முறை மாமன் (*murai maman*) — Cross-cousin (marriageable uncle/cousin)
  - நிஜமா? (*nijama?*) — Really?
  - அப்படீங்களா? (*apadeengala?*) — Is that so? (respectful)
  - நினைச்சேன் (*ninaichen*) — I thought

CALLBACKS:
  - கேட்டேன் (*ketten*) — I asked [struggled]
  - இன்னைக்கு (*innaiku*) — Today [struggled]
  - முதல்ல (*mudalla*) — First [struggled]
  - சொன்னாங்க (*sonnanga*) — They said [mastered]
