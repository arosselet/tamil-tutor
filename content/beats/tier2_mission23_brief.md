# Mission Brief: Tier 2, Mission 23

## Location
The Family House — Living Room (Watching TV)

## Intel Target
The learner must decode a family's emotional reaction to a dramatic movie plot. This is a "Boss Fight" reviewing the emotion vocabulary pattern.

## Word Status Sheet
- **TEACH (The Keys)**: சந்தோஷம் (Happy), கோவம் (Angry)
- **EXPOSE (The Signal)**: பயம் (Scared), கவலை (Worry), சோகம் (Sad)
- **USE**: அண்ணா (Brother), மாமா (Uncle), பாப்பா (Baby), பிடிக்கும் (Like)
- **CALLBACK**: இன்னைக்கு (Today), முன்னாடி (Before)

## Narrative Arc: The Intercept (The Scenario)
1. **The Setup**: Anna, Deepa, and Mama are watching a new Tamil movie on TV. 
2. **The High**: Anna describes the hero getting a promotion. Mama expresses happiness (`சந்தோஷம்`).
3. **The Turn**: The villain threatens the hero's family. Deepa expresses fear (`பயம்`) for the child (`பாப்பா`).
4. **The Betrayal**: The hero's friend betrays him. Anna gets very angry (`கோவம்`).
5. **The Resolution**: They debate if they like the movie. Mama uses the `பிடிக்கும்` (like) pattern to say he loves the action, but feels worry (`கவலை`) about the mother character.

## Narrative Arc: The Breakdown (The Analysis)
1. **Cold Open Teaser**: Maya gives a quick host cue before the Intercept: "You're about to hear a family getting way too invested in a movie plot. Listen for the word that means 'angry'. It's the core emotion of the scene."
2. **Replay Hook**: Raj replays the moment Anna shouts about the betrayal.
3. **The Unpacking**: Maya and Raj translate the entire movie summary line by line.
4. **The Keys**: They isolate `சந்தோஷம்` and `கோவம்`. They discuss the dative pattern naturally ("To me, happiness exists").
5. **Host Quizzing**: Raj playfully tests Maya on what `பயம்` means in the context of the villain scene. They practice the words organically. No meta-narration. No speaking to the audience.
