## Narrative Arc: The Intercept (The Homecoming)

1. **Beating the Heat:** Akka and Thambi are arguing at the **Entrance** (`வாசல்`) as the car pulls up.
2. **The Logistics Struggle:** Mama is shouting from inside the **house** (`வீடு`) about where to put the large trunk.
3. **The Spatial Dispute:** Akka tells Thambi to take the **small** (`சின்ன`) bag **up** (`மேல`), but he says it belongs **down** (`கீழ`).
4. **The Witness:** Thambi claims he **saw** (`பார்த்தேன்`) the bags in the **front** (`முன்னாடி`) of the car, but now they're gone.
5. **The Schedule:** Debate about when they **left** (`கிளம்பு`) and when they need to **return** (`திரும்பு`).

## Narrative Arc: The Breakdown (The Spatial Grid)

1. **The Cold Open Teaser:** *"You're about to hear a family arguing over a suitcase. Listen for the word 'small' — it's the one thing they actually agree on."*
2. **The Replay Hook:** *"Let's go back... 'கீழ இல்ல, மேல வை!' Did you hear that? He's not just complaining, he's giving directions."*
3. **The Spatial Audit:** Maya and Raj unpack `முன்னாடி`, `கீழ`, and `மேல`. They joke about how Coimbatore houses always have "that one room" Upstairs.
4. **The Saw/Seen Pivot:** Unpacking `பார்த்தேன்`. Why it's a "heavy" verb for the learner.
5. **Drill Beat:** Call and response for "First, go home" and "Today, I saw it."
6. **The Closure:** Confirming the learner is now "inside" the house.
