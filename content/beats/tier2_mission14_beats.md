## The Angle & Allocation

- **Vibe:** "The Grocery Run / Long Form Audio". The learner is on a long walk or at the grocery store. This needs to be a steady, immersive narrative that teaches the core "hands-on" verbs of commerce and object manipulation.
- **Rotation Style:** "The Cultural Deep-Dive / The Narrative". We will blend a story about running errands in Coimbatore with the mechanics of the new verbs.
- **Goal:** Master the 5 essential transaction/manipulation verbs (Take, Put, Bring, Give, Buy) while weaving in the recent struggles from the Say/Ask matrix.
