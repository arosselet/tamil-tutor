MISSION BRIEF: Tier 2, Mission 46

BEAT: The Early Market Mission. Andrew needs to reach the Coimbatore flower market before 6 AM to meet his father-in-law's contact. He's groggy, the sun isn't even up, and he's struggling with the sequence of getting out the door. The tension is the ticking clock. He starts hesitant and confused, but by the end, he's fully dressed and out the door ("kilambu"), ready for the street.
