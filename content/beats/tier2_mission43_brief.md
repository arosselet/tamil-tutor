# MISSION BRIEF: Tier 2, Mission 43

BEAT: A chaotic Sunday lunch coordination. Host A is trying to get a straight answer from a group chat and a phone call while the "confirmation window" for a popular biryani spot is closing. Host B is skeptical of the group's ability to commit. What changes: From total confusion to a last-minute "Confirmed" status, but only after a "Did you eat?" check-in.
