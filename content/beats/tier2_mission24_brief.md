# Mission Brief: Tier 2, Mission 24 — The Emotional Audit

**Location:** The Family House — Living Room (Post-Movie Night)
**Intel Target:** Decode the conflicting emotional signals and the "impossible" household requests.

## The Word Status Sheet

- **TEACH (The Keys):** 
    - **பயம்** (Fear/Scared)
    - **கவலை** (Worry)
    - **முடியாது** (Impossible/Cannot)
- **EXPOSE (The Signal):**
    - **தப்பு** (Mistake)
    - **முதல்ல** (First)
    - **பிடிக்காது** (Don't like)
    - **நிமிஷம்** (Minute)
    - **பெட்டி** (Box/Luggage)
- **USE:**
    - **சந்தோஷம்** (Happy)
    - **கோவம்** (Angry)
    - **பிடிக்கும்** (Like)
    - **எனக்கு** (To me)
- **CALLBACK:**
    - **போனேன்** (I went)
    - **சொன்னேன்** (I said)

## Narrative Arc: The Intercept (The Debate Continues)
1. **The Tension:** The movie is over, but the family is still arguing about the plot and the characters.
2. **The Scared Signal:** Deepa explains why she felt **பயம்** (fear) during the climax.
3. **The Mistake:** Anna claims the Hero made a huge **தப்பு** (mistake) by trusting the friend.
4. **The Impossible Request:** Mama asks someone to go to the kitchen, but everyone says **முடியாது** (impossible/cannot) because they are tired.
5. **The Preference:** Everyone debates which part of the movie they **பிடிக்காது** (didn't like).

## Narrative Arc: The Breakdown (The Audit)
1. **The Cold Open:** "Listen for the word that means 'fear'. It’s why the movie night turned into a debate."
2. **The Replay Hook:** "Let's go back to when Deepa said: 'எனக்கு ரொம்ப பயம்'."
3. **The Dative Audit:** Maya and Raj break down the **எனக்கு...** pattern for all the emotions heard (Happy, Angry, Scared, Worried).
4. **The Negative Toggle:** Unpacking **பிடிக்காது** and **முடியாது** — why the "aa" sound at the end is the ultimate "No."
5. **The Sequence:** Explaining why **முதல்ல** (first) was used to prioritize the snacks over the argument.
6. **The Summary:** A final review of the "Threshold Zinger" for the mission.
