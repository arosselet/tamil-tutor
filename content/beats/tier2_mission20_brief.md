# Mission 20 Brief: The Arrival Dispute

**Location:** The Family House — Coimbatore (Living Room)
**Intel Target:** Navigate a friction-filled family discussion about the guest's travel plans and a missing item.

    - `தப்பு` (Wrong / Mistake)
    - `நிஜமா?` (Really? / Is it true?)
- **EXPOSE (The Signal):**
    - `ஏன்?` (Why?)
    - `எப்போ?` (When?)
    - `எப்படி?` (How?)
    - `புரியல` (I don't understand)
    - `சொல்றேன்` (I am saying) - *Struggled word.*
    - `கேக்குறேன்` (I am asking) - *Struggled word.*
    - `கிட்ட` (Near/To) - *Struggled word.*
- **USE (Mastered):**
    - `அக்கா`, `மாமா`, `தம்பி`, `சாப்பிட்டேன்`, `போறேன்`, `இருக்கு`, `சரி`, `ஆமா`.
- **CALLBACK:**
    - `முதல்ல` (First) - *Struggled word.*

## Narrative Arc: The Intercept (The Living Room Friction)

1. **The Tension:** Akka is **angry** (`கோவம்`) because the guest arrived later than expected.
2. **The Accusation:** Mama says it was a **mistake** (`தப்பு`) to take the taxi instead of the bus.
3. **The Inquiry:** Akka asks **when** (`எப்போ`) they **left** (`கிளம்பு`) and **why** (`ஏன்`) they didn't call.
4. **The "I told you so":** Thambi says "I am **saying** (`சொல்றேன்`) the traffic was bad **near** (`கிட்ட`) the station."
5. **The Confusion:** Mama says "I **don't understand** (`புரியல`) **how** (`எப்படி`) it took so long."
6. **The Verification:** Akka asks "Is it **really** (`நிஜமா`) true?" about the traffic.

## Narrative Arc: The Breakdown (Unpacking the Friction)

1. **The Cold Open Teaser:** *"Listen for the word for 'angry'. Someone in this room is definitely losing their cool."*
2. **The Replay Hook:** *"Let's go back... 'அது என் தப்பு இல்ல!' Did you catch that? He's defending himself. 'தப்பு' — mistake."*
3. **The Emotion/Logic Layer:** Unpacking `கோவம்`, `தப்பு`, and `நிஜமா`. How these words drive a dispute.
4. **The Ask/Tell Matrix:** Unpacking `சொல்றேன்` and `கேக்குறேன்`. These were struggled words, now seen in a high-stakes setting.
5. **Drill Beat:** Call and response: "Why are you angry?" and "It was a mistake."
6. **The Closure:** The argument settles as they move towards the kitchen.
