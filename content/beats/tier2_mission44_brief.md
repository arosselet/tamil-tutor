MISSION BRIEF: Tier 2, Mission 44

BEAT: A midday auto ride through Coimbatore. The heat is oppressive (சூடு), and the traffic near the market is chaotic (கஷ்டம்). The driver is talkative, complaining about the weather and the road conditions. The learner must use the new sensation words to empathize and then handle a physical transaction (தூக்கு/வை) once they reach the destination. The tension is the heat—everyone is a bit on edge.

NEW:
  - **சூடு** (*sūḍu*) — Hot (temperature/sensation)
  - **குளிர்** (*kuḷir*) — Cold
  - **கஷ்டம்** (*kaṣṭam*) — Difficult / Hardship
  - **ஈஸி** (*īsī*) — Easy
  - **சரியான** (*sariyāna*) — Correct / Right / Exact

CALLBACKS:
  - **கேட்குறேன்** (*kēṭkuṟēn*) — I am hearing / I am asking [struggled]
  - **கேட்பேன்** (*kēṭpēn*) — I will hear / I will ask [struggled]
  - **தூக்கு** (*thūkku*) — Lift / Pick up [struggled]
  - **வை** (*vai*) — Keep / Put [struggled]
  - **நிஜமா?** (*nijamā?*) — Really? [recently mastered]

NOTES:
  - Focus on the contrast between கேட்குறேன் (present) and கேட்பேன் (future) as recommended in the profile.
  - Land தூக்கு and வை in the context of handling bags at the end of the ride.
  - Andrew tolerates Tamil-heavy stretches, so the Intercept can be immersive.
