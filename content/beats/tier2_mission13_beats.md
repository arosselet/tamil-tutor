# Beat Sheet: Tier 2, Mission 13 - "The Stream (Say & Ask Remix)"

**Style:** The Remix / The Stream (Low Energy, Long, Passive)
**Vibe:** An evening walk in Coimbatore. The air is cooling down. No pressure, no tests. Just the rhythmic hum of the verb engine. The learner already has "Sonnen" locked in. We use that as the anchor to build out the rest of the timeline.
**Target Duration:** 12-15 minutes (Long format).

## Section 1: The Wind Down (Context & Vibe)
- **Beat 1 (Intro):** Host sets the scene. Evening walk. Cool breeze. Drop the shoulders. "You have the past. Now we build the rest."
- **Beat 2 (Anchor):** Host brings back the anchor word. "சொன்னேன்." (I said). It's solid.

## Section 3: The Slow Flow (Saying)
- **Beat 3:** Introduce "சொல்றேன்" (I am saying). Focus on the '-reen' vibration.
- **Beat 4:** The Mantra. Host and Guest exchange slow, rhythmic sentences. "I am saying now."
- **Beat 5 (Callback):** Weave in "இன்னைக்கு" (Today). "Today, I am saying."

## Section 4: The Horizon (Will Say)
- **Beat 6:** Introduce "சொல்லுவேன்" (I will say). Focus on the '-veen' stretch.
- **Beat 7:** The Mantra. "Tomorrow, I will say."
- **Beat 8:** Weaving the timeline. Past -> Present -> Future. (சொன்னேன் -> சொல்றேன் -> சொல்லுவேன்).
- **Beat 9 (Callback):** Weave in "முதல்ல" (First) - "First I said, then I will say."

## Section 5: The Pivot (Ask)
- **Beat 10:** Transition to asking.
- **Beat 11:** Introduce "கேட்டேன்" (I asked). Point out it shares the same ending as "sonnen".
- **Beat 12:** The Mantra. "I asked Anna."

## Section 6: The Present Inquiry
- **Beat 13:** Introduce "கேக்குறேன்" (I am asking). Point out the '-reen' ending matches 'solreen'.
- **Beat 14:** The Mantra. "I am asking you."

## Section 7: The Future Question
- **Beat 15:** Introduce "கேட்பேன்" (I will ask). The '-peen' ending matches '-veen'.
- **Beat 16:** The Mantra. "I will ask later."
- **Beat 17 (Callback):** Weave in "முன்னாடி" (Before) - "Before I asked..."

## Section 8: The Remix Matrix
- **Beat 18:** Very slow, hypnotic repetition crossing the verbs. 
- **Beat 19:** "I said. I asked." (சொன்னேன். கேட்டேன்.)
- **Beat 20:** "I am saying. I am asking." (சொல்றேன். கேக்குறேன்.)
- **Beat 21:** "I will say. I will ask." (சொல்லுவேன். கேட்பேன்.)

## Section 9: The Quiet Fade Out & Zinger
- **Beat 22:** The Zinger. The phrase for walking away. "நான் அப்புறம் சொல்லுவேன்" (I will say later).
- **Beat 23:** Final soothing words. Keep walking. "சரி, போயிட்டு வரேன்."
