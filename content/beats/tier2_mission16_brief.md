## NARRATIVE ARC

### The Intercept — Beat Sheet (Heated Dispute)

1. **The Accusation**: Passenger arrives at platform 3. Porter grabs luggage without asking. Passenger demands the price upfront.
2. **The Overcharge**: Porter names a price that's too high. Passenger refuses — "முடியாது."
3. **The Spatial Manoeuvre**: Porter claims the bags are far below on the trolley and it's heavy work. Language: `கீழ`, `மேல`, `முன்னாடி` embedded naturally in blocking.
4. **The Counter**: Passenger points to the rate board displayed above the counter: `ரேட் போர்டு மேல இருக்கு`. He says he saw (`பார்த்தேன்`) the fixed price. Says he has limited cash (`என் கிட்ட` kammiya irukkku).
5. **The Escalation**: Third party (Tea Stall Owner from Mission 15, referenced but unnamed) calls out from nearby that the fixed rate is indeed lower. Porter goes quiet.
6. **Resolution**: Porter backs down. Passenger pays. Final exchange is curt — modal verbs close the scene.

### The Breakdown — Beat Sheet (Decryption)

**Beat 1 — Replay Hook (Rule 2):**
Analyst Maya opens: "Let's go back to this exact moment — [Porter: இந்த பெட்டி கீழ இருந்துச்சு, மேல தூக்கியேன், அதனால ரேட் ஜாஸ்தி]. Did you catch the three location words in one sentence? That's our crime scene today."

**Beat 2 — The Modal Triad (TEACH):**
Raj breaks down the Can/Cannot/Must Not triad with the Intercept's exact lines. Frame it as the "Power Test" — whoever says முடியாது first is blocking. Whoever says முடியும் is offering. கூடாது is the moral line.

**Beat 3 — The CALLBACK Trio (scene-embedded, not drilled):**
Maya walks through the dispute sequence again: First (`முதல்ல`) the porter moved the bags, before (`முன்னாடி`) the passenger could object. The money was with (`கிட்ட`) the passenger — his leverage. She replays those moments — the words surface from the scene's own logic, never named as review targets.

**Beat 4 — EXPOSE breadcrumbs:**
Raj points out `ஒப்புக்கோ` (the porter's "just accept it" demand) without translating it outright — "you probably figured it out from context. If you didn't, re-listen to the Intercept and see if you get it now. That's how this works."

**Beat 5 — The Zinger:**
The phrase you mutter next time someone tries to overcharge you: **"முடியாது அண்ணா, ரேட் போர்டு பாருங்க."** That's it. That's the whole field test.
