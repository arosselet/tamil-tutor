=== MISSION BRIEF: Tier 2, Mission 33 ===

SEED:
A delivery driver pulls up to a small eatery on a bike to pick up a food order. The owner's wife says it's almost ready — "five more minutes." Twenty minutes later, still waiting. The driver is getting frustrated (other deliveries piling up), the owner's wife is flustered (kitchen is understaffed), and an argument is brewing about who's at fault. Neither is wrong. Both are frustrated. The moment has quiet tension and everyday comedy.

LISTENABILITY:
Anyone who's ever waited for something time-sensitive understands the frustration. The humor isn't contrived — it's the classic "almost ready" that never is, and the clash between two people operating under different pressures. This isn't a vocab dump. It's a real human moment with stakes.

NOT (Last 3 Missions):
  - Mission 31: Tea stall, phone confusion, customer service in a calm setting
  - Mission 32: House hunting, landlord/tenant dynamics, real estate negotiation
  - Mission 30: [Market, buying produce, browsing/selection]

Do NOT write another phone-related scene, another house/property scene, another market/shopping scene. Avoid calm, pleasant interactions. Avoid resolution that's easy or happy. This should have friction.
